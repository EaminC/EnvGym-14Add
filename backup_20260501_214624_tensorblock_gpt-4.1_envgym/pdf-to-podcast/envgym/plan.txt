=== ENVIRONMENT SETUP PLAN (ADJUSTED FOR x86_64 / NO GPU) ===

**This version of the plan is tailored for x86_64 (amd64) CPU architecture with NO NVIDIA GPU present, using Ubuntu 20.04/22.04, and assumes ample CPU/RAM but no CUDA support.
